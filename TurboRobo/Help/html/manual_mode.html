Manual Mode

Start the Simulator. A new window will pop up.



Use the Manual Actions field to perform a movement.

You can choose the duration of the movement in the edit window.

Press on the action you want the robot to perform. The movement will be simulated in speed 10.

The driven course will be tracked. You can hide/display it in the View menu as well as the grid.
